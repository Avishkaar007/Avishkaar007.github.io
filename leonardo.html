Leonardo Da Vinci

Leonardo is identified as one of the greatest painters in the history of art and is often credited as the founder of the High Renaissance.
Leonardo Da Vinci

TIMELINE

  • 15 Apr 1452 : Born in Vinci , a hilltown near Florence ,Italy.

  • 16 Aug 1467 : Leonardo is sent away to study art.

  • 11 Nov 1472 : Leonardo's work was so well received and loved that he was asked to join the Painter's Guild of Florence.

  • 1 Oct 1490 : Started keeping notebooks full of his philosophies and beliefs.
